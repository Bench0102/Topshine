About Us

Our Story

At Tip Top Shine Cleaning, we believe that a clean space is a happy space. Proudly serving homes and businesses for over three years, our journey began with post-construction clean-ups and has since expanded to include residential, commercial, and salon cleaning services.

We're more than just a cleaning company—we're a trusted partner for busy families, business owners, and professionals who value quality, consistency, and exceptional customer service. Our loyal clients choose us because we always show up, shine bright, and make cleanliness stress-free.
